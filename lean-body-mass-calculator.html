What lean body mass includes

Lean body mass is everything that isn't fat — muscle, bone, organs and water. It's a useful baseline for setting protein targets or tracking changes in body composition.

Why the Boer formula

It's one of the more widely validated equations for estimating lean mass from just height, weight and sex — no tape measure or calipers required, though it's still an estimate.

Using this for protein targets

Some people prefer setting protein intake relative to lean mass rather than total bodyweight, especially at higher body fat levels — try the Protein Calculator with this number.

The formula

# Boer formula
Men:
  LBM(kg) = 0.407 × weight(kg) + 0.267 × height(cm) - 19.2

Women:
  LBM(kg) = 0.252 × weight(kg) + 0.473 × height(cm) - 48.3

# Fat mass
Fat mass = weight - LBM
