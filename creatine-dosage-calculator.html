What creatine does

Creatine helps regenerate ATP, your muscles' immediate energy source, supporting a little more output on short, high-intensity efforts like lifting and sprinting.

Loading vs. no loading

A loading phase saturates your muscles with creatine faster, in about a week. Skipping it and going straight to a maintenance dose reaches full saturation too, just more gradually over 3-4 weeks.

Which type of creatine?

Creatine monohydrate is the most researched and cost-effective form, and it's what these dosages assume — other forms haven't shown consistent advantages over it.
