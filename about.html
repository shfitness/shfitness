About us

Free tools, built to be actually useful.

SH Fitness Tools is a collection of calculators for training and nutrition — built to be fast, accurate and free, with no sign-up and no paywalls.

Why we built this

Most fitness calculators online are cluttered with ads, inaccurate formulas, or hidden behind a sign-up wall. We wanted a set of tools that respects your time — enter your numbers, get a clear answer.

How our calculators work

Every calculator uses established, published formulas — the same ones used in strength and conditioning, sports science and clinical nutrition — explained in plain language alongside the result.

Who it's for

Whether you're just starting out or you've been training for years, SH Fitness Tools is built for anyone who wants a quick, reliable number to work from — no account required.

A quick note on accuracy

Every calculator on this site gives a general estimate based on population-level formulas — they're a useful starting point, not a diagnosis or a substitute for professional advice. See our disclaimer for more detail.
